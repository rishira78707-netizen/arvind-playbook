Organisational Framework

Three pillars. One Arvind.

Arvind Limited traces its roots to 1897 as a Lalbhai Group textile venture, formally incorporated as Arvind Mills in 1931. Today it operates across traditional textiles, advanced materials, and diversified segments.

Traditional
Yarn
Yarn Manufacturing
38,000 tonnes per annum. Largest in-house sustainable cotton operations. Impacting 1,00,000+ farmers across 4 lakh acres of farmland.
Fabric
Fabric Production
Denim (60 Mn metres/yr), Wovens (130 Mn metres/yr), Knits (1,200 tonnes/yr), and Voiles (1,200 tonnes/yr) for global markets.
Garments
Garmenting
80 Mn+ pieces annually for global companies. Essentials (35 Mn pcs), Shirts (10 Mn pcs), Jeans (15 Mn pcs).
Voiles
Ankur — Voile Division
Heritage fabric rooted in Arvind's 1930s origins. The Ankur division represents the founding fabric identity of the group.
Modern / Advanced Materials
Composites
Glass & Carbon Fibre
Used in Metro train interiors, wind blades, and sports equipment. High-tech material solutions for industrial and infrastructure sectors.
Human Protection
Protective Wear
Fire, hazardous chemical, and blade-resistant fabrics. Initiated production for the Indian Army.
Industrial
Industrial Products
Conveyor belts, industry filtration systems, and other B2B engineered textile products.
Other Segments
Water
Arvind Envisol — Water Solutions
Environmental stewardship beginning with preserving the planet's most valuable resource — water.
Telecom
Telecom Solutions
Market leader in Mini-EPABXs for SOHO and small-medium businesses across India.
Retail
Arvind Retail
Houses global brands Arrow, US Polo, and the in-house Ready-to-Wear brand "Arvind" under one roof.
Real Estate
Smartspaces
Creating exceptional developments that embody stability and prosperity with lasting value and growth.

Recognition & Engagement

Programmes that see your work.

Arvind has built three distinct programmes to recognise, grow, and engage its people — from peer recognition to structured career mobility.

CLAP
Compliment · Laud · Appreciate · Praise
Arvind's peer recognition programme. Give or receive recognition cards for five defined behaviours. Physical display boards are present across units.
  • Exceptional Performance: When someone turns an idea into exceptional reality
  • Out-of-the-Box Thinking: Fresh ideas that bring new solutions to the table
  • Entrepreneurial Spirit: Taking initiative, solving problems, crushing deadlines proactively
  • Embodies Empathy: Recognising kindness and understanding in daily work
  • Thank You: A simple, meaningful acknowledgement of support
AMBER
CHRO's Virtual Confidante
An AI-powered virtual chatbot for structured feedback — a confidential channel between employees and the CHRO. Feedback is received directly and confidentially by the CHRO.
  • New Joinee: Feedback links sent at 15 days, 45 days, and 90 days after joining
  • Ongoing: Links sent every 6 months thereafter for all employees
  • Confidential: Thoughts shared openly via email link — received only by CHRO
  • Purpose: To give every employee a direct, safe line to leadership
PANKH
Giving Wings to Dreams
Arvind's internal mobility and diversity referral programme — designed to give employees wings to grow within the organisation.
  • Internal Job Posting: Explore internal opportunities that match your skills and expertise for enhanced career growth
  • Employee Referral Programme: Refer a unique talent (female, transgender, or differently-abled) from your network and earn a reward if they are selected as the best fit
